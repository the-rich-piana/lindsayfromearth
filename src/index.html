About Mia

With over 1,500 hours of bodywork training, I offer an integrative, holistic approach to massage, supporting deep physical—and sometimes emotional—release by working with the body, mind, and spirit as a whole. My work focuses on slow, deep techniques that allow the nervous system and fascia to soften and release.

I value both a strong foundation in anatomical knowledge and a deeply present, intentional quality of touch. To me, massage is a beautiful marriage of scientific understanding and intuitive presence.

I love feeling a collaboration with clients and taking the time to understand each individual, their personal goals and circumstances.
