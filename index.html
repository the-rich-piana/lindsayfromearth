About Mia

Welcome! I'm Mia Lindsay, a dedicated practitioner of holistic health and wellness. My journey began over a decade ago when I discovered the transformative power of yoga and mindful movement. Since then, I've devoted myself to helping others find balance, healing, and vitality in their own lives.

With certifications in yoga instruction, therapeutic massage, and holistic health coaching, I bring a comprehensive approach to wellness. I believe that true health comes from nurturing the mind, body, and spirit in harmony. Whether through the flowing movements of yoga, the healing touch of massage, or personalized wellness guidance, my goal is to support you on your unique path to wellbeing.

From the earth, we draw our strength. Let's cultivate your wellness together.

Services

Yoga Instruction

Experience the transformative practice of yoga through personalized instruction. I offer classes in Hatha, Vinyasa, and Restorative yoga, suitable for all levels from complete beginners to advanced practitioners. Each session focuses on breath, alignment, and mindful movement to cultivate strength, flexibility, and inner peace.


  • Private & group sessions

Massage Therapy

Restore balance and release tension through therapeutic massage. My approach combines Swedish, deep tissue, and intuitive bodywork techniques to address your specific needs. Whether you're seeking relief from chronic pain, stress reduction, or simply deep relaxation, each session is tailored to support your healing journey.


  • Therapeutic & deep tissue
  • Swedish relaxation massage
  • Customized treatment plans

Wellness Coaching

Navigate your wellness journey with personalized holistic health coaching. Together, we'll explore nutrition, lifestyle habits, stress management, and self-care practices that align with your values and goals. My approach honors the wisdom of your body and empowers you to make sustainable changes for lasting vitality.


  • Holistic health guidance
  • Nutrition & lifestyle support
  • Sustainable wellness strategies
